SHINSENGUMI

The Shinsengumi is a special police force in Gintama universe. Their main task is to arrest any terrorists especially Jouishishi. Aside from that, they also do regular police duties, and non-terrorist related crimes. Shinsengumi is under control of the Shogunate, therefore they are direct by Police Commissioner Matsudaira. Due to this, sometimes they have to protect Shogun when he goes out.
